The following image shows the resulting spectra: {% include ContentManager.html content=page.dft_interspersed %} After zero padding the inverse DFT is taken using the following formula: $$ \begin{equation} f_n = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{k=0}^{N-1} F_{k}e^{\frac{2\pi ikn}{N}} \end{equation} $$ Finally, the result of the inverse DFT is scaled by a factor of two to account for the zero padding. The following plots show the spectra with the zeros added in the center. {% include ContentManager.html content=page.dft_centered %} As before the inverse DFT is taken and the result is scaled.
